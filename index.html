About Me

My name is Rafeeia Awudu, the heart and hands behind ByFeeyahFaces.

As the first child in my family, I grew up with a natural love for beauty and creativity. I vividly remember sneaking into my mother’s makeup bag as a little girl, experimenting, and even once shaving off my eyebrows only to proudly draw them back on with a pencil. Even then, I knew beauty was about confidence and expression.

In high school, makeup became my passion. I loved helping my friends get ready for events, turning entertainment nights into opportunities to create, experiment, and learn. In university, I saved up to order my very first set of professional makeup products from Nigeria, launching my first real makeup business.

The journey wasn’t a straight line. Life's challenges led me to step away from makeup for three years. However, destiny called me back. One evening, a close friend asked me to do her makeup, and the joy I felt reignited the passion I thought I had lost.

Today, ByFeeyahFaces is a story of resilience, passion, and purpose. Every face I work on is a canvas where I bring out confidence, elegance, and beauty. My dream is to grow ByFeeyah into a global brand that expands into other creative ventures, including fashion and beauty products. Makeup, to me, is about helping people see the best version of themselves, guided by the same fearless spirit of the little girl who once shaved her eyebrows and still believed she looked beautiful.
